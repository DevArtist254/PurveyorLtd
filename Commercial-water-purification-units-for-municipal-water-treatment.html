Commercial water purification units for municipal water treatment

Featured stages of purification:

  • Sediment filtration
  • Organic chemicals, chlorine & odors removal
  • Bacteria removal
  • Water softening
  • Ultraviolet disinfection (UV)

Our plants eliminate most conditions of impurity including sediment, unappetizing taste or odor and bacteriological contamination, the treated water output being to the highest consumption standards.Though the main application is for bottled water production, the plants are also suitable for inline filtration for a distributed supply and also bespoke plants can be designed and manufactured to suit specialized treatment requirements to suits specialized treatment requirements.

Optional features

  • Remote control
  • UV sterilization
  • Chlorination
  • Dechlorination
  • PH adjust
  • Media filters (sand, glass, activate carbon )
  • Ion exchanger
  • Corrosion inhibitor
