RO for hospital dialysis systems

Features

  • Stainless Steel Multistage Pump
  • Stainless-Steel Pressure Vessels
  • PLC driven control panel
  • Permeate/Concentrate Flowmeters
  • High and Low Pressure Protection
  • 5 Micron Sediment Pre-filter

Dialysis machines need ultra pure water to prevent patients from getting infections from microorganisms in the water. That’s why advances created reverse osmosis systems for dialysis machines. The ultra pure water needs vary depending on the capacity of the dialysis center and treatment of each center. However Purveyor had delivered two main reverse osmosis systems for dialysis to cover all needs for this high standards of water quality. Our RO Dialysis Water treatment Unit provides the required high standards water quality for any Dialysis center or hospital. The sizing of our equipment can be adapted based on the patient population of the center.

Optional features

  • Remote control
  • UV sterilization
  • Chlorination
  • Dechlorination
  • PH adjust
  • Media filters (sand, glass, activate carbon )
  • Ion exchanger
  • Corrosion inhibitor
  • Turbidity monitor
